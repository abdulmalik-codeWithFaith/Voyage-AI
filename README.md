# 🗺️ Voyage AI

> **Travel Smarter. Spend Less. Arrive Better.**

Voyage AI is an AI-powered travel planning assistant that helps users choose the best way to travel between two locations based on cost, travel time, convenience, and personal preferences. Instead of simply providing directions, Voyage AI compares multiple transportation options and recommends the smartest choice for every journey.

---

# 🌍 The Problem

Planning a trip often requires checking multiple apps to compare transportation options, costs, travel times, and routes. Travelers frequently struggle to answer questions like:

* Which transport option is the cheapest?
* Which one gets me there the fastest?
* How much fuel will I spend if I drive?
* Is taking a bus or a flight more economical?
* Which route is best based on my budget and schedule?

This process is time-consuming and inconvenient.

---

# 💡 The Solution

Voyage AI acts as your intelligent travel decision assistant.

Simply enter your current location and destination, with optional details like budget, number of travelers, or departure time. Voyage AI compares multiple travel options—including walking, motorcycles, buses, taxis, personal vehicles, trains, and flights—and recommends the best choice based on your needs.

Every recommendation includes estimated travel time, cost, fuel consumption (where applicable), advantages, disadvantages, and a one-click navigation link that opens directly in Google Maps.

# 🚀 How It Works

1. Enter your current location.
2. Enter your destination.
3. Optionally provide:

   * Budget
   * Number of travelers
   * Departure time
4. Voyage AI analyzes available transportation options.
5. The AI generates:

   * Estimated travel times
   * Estimated costs
   * Fuel costs (if driving)
   * Route comparison
   * Best transportation recommendation
6. Tap **View Route** to open Google Maps navigation.
7. Download your travel report.
